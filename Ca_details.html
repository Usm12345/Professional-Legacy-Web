CA Pakistan typically refers to the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Pakistan (ICAP). ICAP is a professional accountancy body in Pakistan that was established in 1961. It is responsible for regulating the accountancy profession in the country and providing education and training to aspiring Chartered Accountants.



Eligibility:
