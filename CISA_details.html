The Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA) certification is a globally recognized certification issued by The Information System Audit and Control Association (ISACA). The CISA certification serves as a widely accepted standard for evaluating the knowledge, training, and capacity of IT auditors to carry out IT security measures within an organisation.
